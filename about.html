    Principles Of Siddha Medicine
    All the existing things in this world are made up of 5 elements namely : Earth, Water, Fire, Air and Space , which are called the Fundamental Boothas(Elements). The origin of the five elements and the formation of different substances in the Universe by various combinations of the elements are clearly described in Siddha Medicine. The disturbance or ups & downs of these elements is known as dhosham (thiri dhosham-VATHAM - space and air; PITHAM-fire and water; KABAM - earth and water ) which leads to diseases according to siddha medicine.
    Principles Of Ayurveda Medicine
    Ayurveda is an ancient Indian system of medicine that emphasizes the balance of three fundamental energies, known as doshas—Vata (air and space), Pitta (fire and water), and Kapha (earth and water). These doshas govern all physical and mental processes and their balance is essential for good health. The origin of diseases in Ayurveda is believed to be the imbalance of these doshas, and treatment focuses on restoring balance through diet, herbal remedies, lifestyle changes, and other holistic practices.
    Principles Of Yoga
    Yoga is a holistic discipline from ancient India that combines physical postures (asanas), breath control (pranayama), and meditation (dhyana) to harmonize the body, mind, and spirit. The ultimate goal of yoga is to achieve a state of self-realization and inner peace. It is based on the principle that the body is made up of five elements—earth, water, fire, air, and space—and by balancing these elements through yoga practices, one can achieve physical health and mental well-being.
    Principles Of Homeopathy Medicine
    Homeopathy is a system of alternative medicine based on the principle of "like cures like," meaning that a substance that causes symptoms in a healthy person can be used to treat similar symptoms in a sick person, but in very small, diluted amounts. It emphasizes the body’s natural ability to heal itself and focuses on treating the individual as a whole, rather than just the symptoms of a disease. The remedies are derived from natural substances, including plants, minerals, and animals, and are tailored to each person’s unique constitution.
    Principles Of Naturopathy Medicine
    Naturopathy is a holistic approach to healthcare that focuses on natural remedies and the body's innate ability to heal itself. It combines traditional therapies with modern science and emphasizes prevention, treatment, and optimal health through natural means. Naturopathy is based on several principles, including the healing power of nature, identifying and treating the root cause of illness, and treating the whole person (body, mind, and spirit). The treatments may include diet and nutrition, herbal medicine, exercise, stress management, and other lifestyle changes to promote balance and well-being.
